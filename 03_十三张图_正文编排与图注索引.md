## 1. 整组图的叙事弧线

十三张图应共同回答一个问题：为什么“拥有大量档案”不等于“拥有可训练、可回放的 decision traces”，以及这一缺口为何迫使历史结果模型转变为权限受限的证据、注意力和 prospective trace-production 系统？

建议顺序为：

```text
原始档案可恢复 partial trace fragments
→ 170列是trace inventory而非170个输入
→ 历史trace coverage呈分层稀疏
→ effort/action与tool traces主要缺在哪里
→ reasonable-effort adequacy 无法直接监督
→ historical difficulty 存在梯度
→ 先选参照组再比较
→ pooled threshold 的审查负担集中
→ 108→17 的trace-compatible输入收缩
→ A排队、B选尺、规则提示、人终裁并记录理由
→ 性能边界迫使系统限权
→ 词表缺口与prospective decision-trace schema
```

## 3. 逐图图注与允许结论

### Fig. 1 — 从原始档案重建 partial decision-trace fragments

**源文件**：`../efforts_and_tools_distributon/figures/fig1_provenance_map.pdf`

**中文图注**：从结果导向档案重建可追溯 trace fragments 的多模态管线及不同分析范围。635 个含 PDF 的历史案卷（约 1,700 份 PDF）与 legacy register 经候选抽取、数值/证据/时序 guard、quarantine 和 canonical merge 进入研究表；不同 teacher、DN 和 form-model 分支使用预定义子集。视觉字段保留文件、页码、原文和时间属性；部分井属性与标签来自 legacy register 或确定性派生台账。该管线恢复已记录片段，不声称恢复未记录的 deliberation 或完整 decision trace。

**English caption**: Multimodal reconstruction of provenance-bearing trace fragments and nonexchangeable analytic scopes. A corpus of 635 PDF-backed folders (approximately 1,700 PDFs) and a legacy register passed through candidate extraction, numeric/evidence/temporal guards, quarantine, and canonical merging before predefined subsets were used by teacher, DN, and form-model analyses. Visual fields retained file, page, quotation, and timing; some attributes originated in the register or deterministic ledgers. The pipeline recovers recorded fragments, not unrecorded deliberation or a complete decision trace.

**证明的观点**：多模态是 partial-trace reconstruction 的测量需要；可疑字段被隔离而非静默进入模型；provenance 是输出的一部分。

**不能写**：“每个字段都来自某一 PDF 页”“635 案都进入同一个模型”“抽取已通过外部专家验证”。

### Fig. 2 — 完整 schema 与模型输入不是同一件事

**源文件**：`../efforts_and_tools_distributon/figures/fig2_model_feature_composition.pdf`

**中文图注**：canonical schema 的语义组成及模型特征子集。170 列包括 depth 41、effort/struggle 34、tools/remediation 18、admin/docs 16、well covariates 16、obstruction 10、checklist 16、outcome 7 和 identity/provenance 12。archival DN、teacher 和 request-time-compatible form models 分别使用 108、68 和 17 项子集；checklist、outcome 和 provenance 列不应被误称为 serving features。

**English caption**: Semantic composition of the canonical schema and predefined model subsets. The 170 columns comprised 41 depth, 34 effort/struggle, 18 tool/remediation, 16 administrative/document, 16 well-covariate, 10 obstruction, 16 checklist, seven outcome, and 12 identity/provenance variables. Archival DN, teacher, and request-time-compatible form models used distinct 108-, 68-, and 17-variable subsets; checklist, outcome, and provenance columns were not serving features.

**证明的观点**：170 列是审计和 partial-trace inventory，不是完整 trace；17 字段是有意识的 request-time 输入治理。

**必须限定**：108-feature archival DN 包含 certificate/completion availability，只能用于档案研究。

### Fig. 3 — 历史 decision-trace coverage 的分层稀疏性

**源文件**：`../efforts_and_tools_distributon/figures/fig3_sparsity_atlas.pdf`

**中文图注**：424 案研究 cohort 中的档案覆盖层级。行政/井属性通常覆盖 90%–96%，申请叙述约 30%–41%，深度证据约 15%–30%，而细粒度 work-record difficulty、remedy 和 obstruction flags 几乎均低于 2.5%。该图显示的是当前档案和抽取台账中的记录可得性，不是事件真实发生率。

**English caption**: Documentation strata in the 424-case research cohort. Administrative and well attributes generally covered 90%–96% of cases, request narratives approximately 30%–41%, and depth evidence approximately 15%–30%, whereas fine-grained work-record difficulty, remedy, and obstruction flags were almost all below 2.5%. Values represent availability in the archive and extraction ledger, not underlying event incidence.

**证明的观点**：trace 缺失高度结构化；行政状态饱和不能弥补 effort/action evidence 数据荒漠。

**不能写**：“作业事件本身不足 2.5%”。424 案有 2,874 条带日期事件；低于 2.5% 指细粒度 flags。

### Fig. 4 — 困难证据的两种记录层

**源文件**：`../efforts_and_tools_distributon/figures/fig4_efforts_distribution.pdf`

**中文图注**：申请叙述与独立 work-record aggregation 中困难概念的记录率。cave-in/collapse 为 17.45% 对 0.24%，hard fill/no progress 为 12.5% 对 2.36%，stuck/lost tools 为 5.90% 对 1.89%，casing failure 为 3.77% 对 1.18%；有非零分母的差异约 3–74 倍，mine-through 为 7.31% 对 0。差异反映文档记录/抽取层，而非 operator 实际行为或努力程度。

**English caption**: Documentation prevalence of difficulty concepts in request narratives versus separately aggregated work records. Cave-in/collapse was recorded in 17.45% versus 0.24%, hard fill/no progress in 12.5% versus 2.36%, stuck/lost tools in 5.90% versus 1.89%, and casing failure in 3.77% versus 1.18%; finite ratios ranged approximately 3–74, while mine-through was 7.31% versus zero. Differences describe documentation or extraction layers, not operator behavior or effort.

**证明的观点**：结果支持把 DN 定义为 documented difficulty，而不是实际 effort adequacy。

**不能写**：“operator 到申请时才开始描述困难”或“工作日志证明没有努力”。

### Fig. 5 — 工具和补救方法的记录荒漠

**源文件**：`../efforts_and_tools_distributon/figures/fig5_tools_distribution.pdf`

**中文图注**：2,874 条带日期事件中的工具与 deployment-method 记录。197 条事件具有任何工具 token，故 93.15% 无 token；2,687 条 deployment method 为 unknown，故 93.49% 未知。169 个原始 token 包含同义词、粒度混杂和 OCR 噪声；remediation flags 在 work-record 层均不超过 0.71%。

**English caption**: Tool and deployment-method documentation among 2,874 dated events. Any tool token appeared in 197 events, leaving 93.15% without a token; deployment method was unknown in 2,687 events (93.49%). The 169 raw strings included synonyms, mixed granularity, and OCR noise, while work-record remediation flags were each no greater than 0.71%.

**证明的观点**：当前历史数据不足以可靠训练 tool/remedy signal；生产表单应前瞻结构化收集。

**不能写**：“93% 的作业没有使用工具”或“存在 169 种独立工具”。

### Fig. 6 — Effort/action traces 过于稀疏，无法监督 reasonable-effort adequacy

**源文件**：`../Model A and B role/figures/fig1_effort_documentation_scarcity.pdf`

**中文图注**：424 案中的 effort-related evidence coverage。99.8% 至少有一条带日期台账记录，但 explicit attempted-and-failed evidence 仅 6.1%，obstruction depth 14.6%，maximum reached 18.9%，effort/work dated events 21.7%，项目内部裁决 DN 为 112/424（26.4%）。G6 的 15 案随机小样本中 pre-request effort density 为 0/15。该图支持“直接监督 effort adequacy 的信号极稀缺”，不证明总体绝对为零。

**English caption**: Coverage of effort-related evidence in 424 cases. Although 99.8% had at least one dated ledger row, explicit attempted-and-failed evidence appeared in 6.1%, obstruction depth in 14.6%, maximum reached depth in 18.9%, effort/work dated events in 21.7%, and internally adjudicated DN in 112/424 (26.4%). A 15-case G6 random audit found zero pre-request effort density. The figure supports severe signal scarcity, not proof of a population value of zero.

**证明的观点**：difficulty 可以有粗粒度文档代理，adequacy 不能从历史结果态直接学得；99.8% 有台账不等于拥有完整 action trace。

### Fig. 7 — 信号集中与 108→17 输入治理

**源文件**：`../efforts_and_tools_distributon/figures/fig7_signal_concentration.pdf`

**中文图注**：archival DN model 中的特征信号集中及 request-time 输入收缩。108 项 full-refit permutation importance 中仅 7 项报告为正；108-feature archival HGB 的内部 grouped AUC 为 0.8412（fold SD 0.1136），17-field Model A 为 0.8366（fold SD 0.1097）。数值是内部点估计且未做 equivalence test；101 项约为零不表示字面恒零或无因果作用。

**English caption**: Feature-signal concentration in the archival DN model and contraction to request-time-compatible inputs. Only seven of 108 full-refit permutation importances were reported as positive. Internally grouped AUC was 0.8412 (fold SD 0.1136) for the 108-feature archival HGB and 0.8366 (fold SD 0.1097) for 17-field Model A. These are internal point estimates without an equivalence test; near-zero importance does not imply literal constancy or absence of causal relevance.

**证明的观点**：在当前 DN 任务中，稀疏档案细项没有带来明显点估计增益；17 字段避免 post-work inputs，但只覆盖 decision trace 的小部分。

**不能写**：“17 字段已证明对 Model B 或所有未来任务都足够”。

### Fig. 8 — 两个模型的内部性能与后果边界

**源文件**：`../Model A and B role/figures/fig6_model_performance_boundaries.pdf`

**中文图注**：Model A 与 Model B 的内部 out-of-fold 表现和允许用途。Model A 的官方 DN-ranking AUC 为 0.8366（fold SD 0.1097），用于 documented-difficulty trace priority；Model B 与 locked teacher 的五折 county-grouped Spearman 为 0.418（fold SD 0.047），exact-bin agreement 35.5%、within-one-bin 72.3%，用于 historical-reference selection。两者是功能分工，不是 effort 与 geography 的因果分解；均不支持 adequacy、objective difficulty、approval 或 refusal。

**English caption**: Internal out-of-fold performance and consequence boundaries. Model A achieved DN-ranking AUC 0.8366 (fold SD 0.1097) for documented-difficulty trace priority. Model B achieved five-fold county-grouped Spearman 0.418 (fold SD 0.047) against the locked teacher, with 35.5% exact-bin and 72.3% within-one-bin agreement, for historical-reference selection. This functional division is not causal factorization of effort and geography and supports neither adequacy nor approval/refusal.

**必须限定**：AUC 不是 accuracy；B 的 ±0.047 不是 repeated-CV uncertainty 或 CI；27.7% 相差至少两档。

### Fig. 9 — teacher-defined historical difficulty gradient

**源文件**：`../Model A and B role/figures/fig2_difficulty_gradient.pdf`

**中文图注**：354 个 operationally defined non-audited-refused 历史参考记录在 locked-teacher strata 中的结果态 shortfall 与 DN 梯度。每档 70–71 条；历史 `label_bottom_of_plug` shortfall 中位数从 Q1 的 0.037 增至 Q5 的 0.281（7.6 倍），DN 从 8.5% 增至 40.8%（4.8 倍）。分档由 teacher 而非 Model B OOF proxy 给出；该关联不是客观工程难度或法律充分性的外部验证。

**English caption**: Outcome shortfall and DN gradients across locked-teacher strata in 354 operationally defined non-audited-refused historical reference records. Each stratum contained 70–71 records. Median shortfall based on `label_bottom_of_plug` rose from 0.037 in Q1 to 0.281 in Q5 (7.6-fold), while DN prevalence rose from 8.5% to 40.8% (4.8-fold). Strata were assigned by the teacher rather than the Model-B OOF proxy and do not externally validate objective engineering difficulty or legal adequacy.

**提交前图面修订**：若现图写 “approved cases”，改成 “operationally defined historical reference records”；若暗示 Model B 分档，改成 teacher-defined strata。

### Fig. 10 — 同一 request shortfall 在不同参照档中的不同含义

### Fig. 12 — 双模型与混合权限架构

**源文件**：`../Model A and B role/figures/fig5_two_model_architecture.pdf`

**中文图注**：Model A、Model B、规则、人和 prospective trace ledger 的概念性分权。Model A 只排序 review queue；Model B 选择参照档，并经 in-bin tail 间接影响 preliminary route；checklist 和 compliance rules 使明确问题可见；合格审查员核验来源并承担最终行动；版本化 ledger 保存 rule、revision、field acceptance、reason、override 和 later outcome。该图描述目标架构，不表示当前代码已强制完整 trace capture。

### Fig. 13 — training–serving 词表缺口

**源文件**：`../efforts_and_tools_distributon/figures/fig6_vocab_alignment.pdf`

**中文图注**：历史抽取词表与生产 intake 词表的对齐状态。六类 STRUGGLES 逐字对齐，七类 REMEDIES 是历史八类的子集，九类 DN_TYPES 来自 checklist 类型学；但历史三类 phase 与 intake 十类 EVENT_TYPES 没有 crosswalk。历史 `n_effort_work_events` 与 serving `n_effort_events` 名称相近但语义不完全同构。

**English caption**: Alignment between historical extraction and production-intake vocabularies. Six STRUGGLES values aligned directly, seven REMEDIES values formed a subset of eight historical values, and nine DN_TYPES came from the checklist taxonomy; however, no crosswalk connected the three historical phases to the ten intake EVENT_TYPES. Historical `n_effort_work_events` and serving `n_effort_events` have similar names but nonidentical semantics.

**证明的观点**：historical event vocabulary 与 prospective trace schema 尚不一致；外部试点前必须由领域专家建立 3→10 crosswalk，并用首批真实 intake 验证定义和漂移。

## 5. 一句汇报总括

> 十三张图共同说明：档案碎片可以被重建为 partial decision traces，并支持有限排序和参照选择；但 action/tool traces 的结构性稀缺、teacher-to-proxy 误差、结果态迁移和失败门控，使模型既不能反推出 reasonable-effort adequacy，也不能接管审批责任。系统下一阶段的价值是前瞻生产可审计 traces，而不是补造历史原因。
